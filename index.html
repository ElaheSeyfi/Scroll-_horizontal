VECTOR'S MORE THAN A HOME ROBOT.

TAKE A PHOTO

Ask him to take a picture, say "cheese", and wait while he snaps it. Can we call this a robot selfie?

BLACKJACK

He's a robot. He's a friend. He's a blackjack dealer. He'll bring a smile to your face

A HOME ROBOT. Alive with personality, he is excited to meet you and hang out. Vector is happiest when he’s helping. He’s eager to accommodate your requests and answer your questions.

He isn’t a fully grown robot butler capable of doing your taxes, buttering your bread, or writing a position paper on the future of robot/human relationships, but he’s a helpful little guy who puts his whole self into helping you out.

WEATHER

Ask him any city's weather and he'll show you.

TIMER

Vector will set a timer and share in your joy when your muffins come out perfect, or when your laundry is finally done.
